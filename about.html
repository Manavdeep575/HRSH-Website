About HRSH

Built for Businesses That Are Going Places.

HRSH was built for businesses at the growth stage where HR stops being optional and starts being the thing that determines how well you can keep up with your own momentum.

Our Story

Why HRSH Exists

Most HR support is designed for businesses that already have everything figured out. HRSH was built for the ones still in motion: growing fast, hiring hard, and needing HR that can keep up.

Harsh founded HRSH because she saw firsthand what the right HR infrastructure unlocks at this stage: faster hiring, stronger retention, cleaner compliance, and a team that performs at its best. She built HRSH to make that accessible to the businesses that need it most.

She started HRSH to bring exactly that to businesses at the stage where it matters most.

HG

The Founder

Harsh Gill, CHRP

Founder

I'm Harsh Gill, a certified HR professional with over five years of experience working directly alongside executive teams inside high-growth, multi-regional companies in fast-paced industries.

That proximity gave me something I carry into every engagement: a clear understanding of what actually drives a business and what its people function needs to do to support it. I've built HR functions from scratch, scaled them, and earned executive buy-in by delivering outcomes that showed up in the numbers. A 15% improvement in retention. A 10-point increase in engagement scores. A leadership program where half the participants were promoted within six months.

I don't come in with a templated solution. I start by understanding your business, then build what it actually needs, automated so your operations stay lean as you grow.

What I've seen is that the businesses that grow well are the ones that treat HR as a driver, not an afterthought. HRSH exists to make that possible for businesses at exactly this stage.
