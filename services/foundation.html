HR Foundations

The HR Foundation Your Business Needs.

Before you can build for what's next, you need to know exactly where you stand today. HR Foundations starts with a complete audit of your current state, then builds and automates the infrastructure your team needs to run efficiently as it grows.

HR Foundations is designed for businesses that are past the early startup stage and ready to build something that actually holds up: a properly structured HR function that runs lean, stays compliant, and doesn't require a full-time HR team to maintain.

1

HR Audit & Compliance Review

We start by looking at everything: your existing policies, employee handbook, HR processes, and operational workflows. The goal is to give you a picture of what's working, what's missing, and what's creating risk.

From there, we identify the compliance gaps, the process inefficiencies, and the priorities, so the build phase is focused on what actually matters.

Included:

Policy and handbook review
Compliance gap analysis
Process and workflow audit
Risk identification and prioritization
Audit summary report with recommendations
2

Policy & Handbook Development

We build or rebuild the policy framework your business needs to operate confidently. That means an employee handbook your team will actually use, policies that reflect your culture and meet your legal obligations, and documentation that's clear, current, and consistently applied.

Included:

Employee handbook (full build or refresh)
Core HR policies
Employment standards alignment
Policy communication templates
3

Process Architecture

We map and redesign the HR processes that run your business day to day: onboarding, offboarding, performance management, leave administration, and more. Every process is documented, standardized, and built to be handed off to a manager or automated entirely.

Included:

Onboarding and offboarding workflow design
Performance management process documentation
Leave and accommodation process maps
Manager-ready toolkits and templates
Standard operating procedures for core HR functions
4

HR Technology & Automation

We assess your current tech stack and identify where automation can reduce manual work, improve consistency, and free up your time. Whether we're optimizing what you already have or helping you select and implement the right tools, the goal is an HR function that runs efficiently without constant oversight.

Included:

HRIS and tool stack assessment
Automation opportunity mapping
Workflow automation setup and integration
Tech implementation support
System documentation and training

Who This Is For

HR Foundations is the right starting point if your business is growing and you need to get your HR house in order, whether that means building from scratch, cleaning up what's already there, or replacing manual processes with something that actually runs itself.
