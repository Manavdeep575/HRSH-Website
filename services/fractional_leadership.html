Fractional HR Leadership & Support

Senior HR Expertise, When You Need It

Not every business at this stage needs a full-time HR leader. But every business at this stage needs access to one. Fractional HR Leadership gives you the strategic thinking, experienced judgment, and hands-on support of a seasoned HR professional, on a schedule and scope that fits where you actually are.

This service is for businesses that have built some HR infrastructure, or are building it with HRSH, and need ongoing expert support to navigate the bigger decisions and more complex situations that come with growth. Think of it as having a senior HR leader embedded in your business, without the overhead of a full-time hire.

1

Strategic HR Advisory

Growth creates decisions that don't have obvious answers: when to hire, how to restructure, how to handle a senior departure, what your compensation philosophy should be. We bring experienced HR perspective to those decisions, helping you think through the people implications before they become expensive problems.

Included:

Ongoing strategic HR advisory and thought partnership
People planning aligned to business goals
HR roadmap development and prioritization
Leadership team coaching on people matters
2

Complex Employee Relations Support

Some people situations require more than a process; they require experienced judgment. Investigations, performance management, difficult terminations, accommodation disputes, team dynamics issues: these are the moments where having a seasoned HR professional in your corner makes a material difference to both the outcome and your exposure.

Included:

Complex workplace investigation support
Senior employee performance and exit management
Termination risk assessment and process guidance
Human rights and accommodation advisory
Sensitive team dynamics and conflict navigation
3

Compliance & Policy Oversight

Employment law doesn't stand still, and neither does your workforce. We keep your HR policies and practices current, reviewing and updating documentation as legislation changes, flagging emerging compliance risks, and ensuring your people processes hold up to scrutiny. For businesses operating across multiple provinces or jurisdictions, we provide oversight that keeps everything aligned.

Included:

Ongoing policy review and compliance monitoring
Employment standards updates and implementation
Multi-jurisdiction compliance alignment
HR audit support and remediation
Proactive risk flagging and recommendations

Who This Is For

Fractional HR Leadership is the right fit if your business has HR infrastructure in place, and needs ongoing expert support to handle the situations and decisions that go beyond what a junior HR person or a process document can address. It's also the right fit if you're between HR hires and need experienced coverage while you figure out what full-time HR should look like for your next stage.
