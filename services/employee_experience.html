Employee Experience & Relations

A Workplace People Thrive In

Culture is easy to maintain when a team is small. The challenge is keeping it intact as headcount climbs, roles diversify, and the informal connective tissue that worked at 10 people stops working at 40. Employee Experience & Relations builds the infrastructure that holds your culture together and handles people challenges before they become business problems.

This service is for businesses that want to take ownership of how their team experiences work. We build the engagement frameworks, communication systems, and employee relations processes that keep your people connected, your culture strong, and your leadership equipped to handle what comes up.

1

Engagement Strategy

You can't improve what you don't measure. We design, implement and, analyze an engagement survey to give you a strategy plan. The goal is to give you a real, ongoing read on how your team is doing, with a clear action plan for what to do with that information. It's a process that builds trust, surfaces issues early, and gives leaders the data they need to make better decisions.

Included:

Engagement survey design and cadence
Stay interview frameworks
Results analysis and action planning
Manager-ready communication guides
Pulse check templates for ongoing monitoring
2

Organizational Design

How your business is structured has a direct impact on how well it performs. As teams grow, reporting lines blur, roles overlap, and the org chart that made sense at 15 people stops working at 40. We help you design organizational structures that are clear, efficient, and built to support your next stage, without the political baggage that often comes with restructuring.

Included:

Org structure assessment and redesign
Role clarity and accountability mapping
Spans and layers analysis
Restructuring planning and change communication
Job architecture and levelling framework
3

Employee Relations Frameworks

Employee relations issues are inevitable. What matters is whether your business has a consistent, documented way to handle them. We build the frameworks your managers need to navigate performance concerns, workplace conflicts, and sensitive situations: fairly, consistently, and in a way that protects both the employee and the business.

Included:

Conflict resolution process and manager guide
Progressive discipline framework and documentation
Workplace investigation protocol
Accommodation and duty-to-accommodate process
HR escalation pathways and decision trees
4

Culture & Communication Infrastructure

Culture at a growing company doesn't maintain itself; it needs active investment and consistent communication. We help you define what your culture actually is, build the internal communication rhythms that reinforce it, and create the touchpoints that keep your team feeling connected regardless of team size or working arrangement.

Included:

Culture assessment and values articulation
Internal communication calendar and templates
Recognition program design
Onboarding culture integration
Team ritual and rhythm design (town halls, check-ins, milestones)
5

Exit & Offboarding Experience

How a business handles departures says as much about its culture as how it handles new hires. We build a structured offboarding process that protects your business, captures knowledge before it walks out the door, and leaves departing employees with a genuinely good final impression as your alumni are part of your employer brand.

Included:

Exit interview process and question bank
Knowledge transfer documentation framework
Offboarding checklist and timeline
Alumni relations approach
Exit data analysis and reporting

Who This Is For

Employee Experience & Relations is the right fit if your team has grown to the point where culture feels less consistent than it used to, or if you're building the people infrastructure before issues start showing up. It's also the right fit if you've had employee relations situations that exposed gaps in how your business handles conflict, performance, or sensitive workplace matters.
